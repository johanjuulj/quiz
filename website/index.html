About

Fishy was developed to help fishermen do what they are good at.. fish. We do the paperwork, registration and put things in order.

Track your catch

Your catch is unique every day. It changes all the time. Tracking it and labeling everything in accordance with the law is a tedious job. We can do all that for you and more.

Register

We register your catch with the authorities automatically. You don't need to do anything.

Accounting

We even intrate with your own and your buyers accounting software and ensure everything is registered accordingly. + We even make it easy to send out invoices.

Services

3 clicks and all your paperwork is gone!.
